Ordering LiDAR from ELVIS

  1. Click Download Shapefile above to save your study area boundary as a WGS84 .zip.
  2. Click Open ELVIS Portal, then click Order Data in the top navigation bar.
  3. Click Load File, then upload the shapefile .zip you downloaded above — the portal will zoom to your area and load available data.
  4. Click Search, then under NSW Government – Spatial Services → Point Clouds, click Select All. Note: LiDAR coverage varies — not all regions will have point cloud data available.
  5. Fill out the Industry and Email fields and click Order. You will receive an email with a download link — download the .zip file and proceed to Step 3.
